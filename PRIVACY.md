# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 12, 2026
**Operated by:** Brand Anthony McDonald ("BAM", "I", "me")

This privacy policy describes how information is collected, used, and stored when you visit this website.

---

## Information We Collect

### Contact Form
When you submit the contact form, I collect:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your message

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

### Client Portal
If you access a client project portal, I collect:
- Your email address (for authentication)
- Access logs (timestamps, actions taken within the portal)

### Analytics
This site uses **Vercel Analytics**, which collects anonymized, aggregated usage data (page views, referrers, device type). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

### Google reCAPTCHA v3
The contact form uses **Google reCAPTCHA v3** to detect spam and abuse. reCAPTCHA collects hardware and software information and sends it to Google. Google's use of this data is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Education Submissions
If you submit an educational inquiry or download the Corvids e-book, I may collect your name, email, and submission details to respond or fulfill the request.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **Respond to contact form submissions** — I use your name and email to reply to your message.
- **Authenticate client portal access** — Email-based authentication to grant project-specific access.
- **Improve the site** — Aggregated analytics help identify what content is useful.
- **Prevent abuse** — reCAPTCHA data helps protect the contact form from spam.

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## Data Storage

- **Contact submissions and portal data** are stored in a MongoDB Atlas database hosted on cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest.
- **Images and media** are hosted on Cloudinary's secure CDN.
- **Emails** are transmitted via Gmail SMTP (Google's servers) for delivery.

## Cookies and Sessions

This site uses cookies strictly for functionality:

- **NextAuth session cookies** — Used to maintain authenticated sessions for the client portal and admin dashboard. These are session-scoped and expire when you close your browser or after a set timeout.
- **JWT tokens** — Used for client portal authentication. Tokens are short-lived and scoped to specific projects.

No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

## Data Retention

- **Contact form submissions** are retained as long as they are relevant for business purposes and deleted upon request.
- **Client portal access data** is retained for the duration of the project engagement and deleted upon project closure or upon request.
- **Analytics data** (Vercel) is aggregated and anonymized; no retention period applies to individual users.
